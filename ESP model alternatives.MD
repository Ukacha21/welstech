For a smartwatch project, you need a **compact ESP32 module** that supports **low power consumption**, **small form factor**, and **easy reprogramming**. Here are some great options:

### **Best ESP32 Models for Smartwatch Projects**
#### 1️⃣ **ESP32-S3 Mini**
   - ✅ **Ultra-small** (18mm × 25mm)
   - ✅ **Low power mode** (deep sleep support)
   - ✅ **USB Type-C for programming** (no need for an FTDI adapter)
   - ✅ **Better power efficiency**
   - ⚡ **More GPIOs available** compared to ESP32-C3

#### 2️⃣ **ESP32-C3 Mini**
   - ✅ **RISC-V processor** (efficient and small)
   - ✅ **USB direct flashing** (easier than ESP32-CAM)
   - ✅ **Lower power than standard ESP32**
   - ✅ **Built-in BLE + WiFi**

#### 3️⃣ **ESP32-PICO-D4**
   - ✅ **Extremely small (7×7mm)**
   - ✅ **Low power and energy-efficient**
   - ✅ **Good for battery-powered wearables**
   - ❌ Harder to solder since it's an SMT module

#### 4️⃣ **ESP32-S3-WROOM-1**
   - ✅ **More powerful (Dual-core)**
   - ✅ **Native USB support**
   - ✅ **Good balance of size & power**
   - ❌ Slightly bigger than other options
